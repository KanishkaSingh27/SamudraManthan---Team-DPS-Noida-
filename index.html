About Us

Our business monitors and explores undersea environments using satellite imagery and biomimicry fish technology. Our cutting-edge robotic fish gather critical data on temperature, pollution, and water quality thanks to their sophisticated sensors and AI-driven autonomy. Making educated decisions is made possible by the extensive coverage that real-time satellite photography offers. Our data analytics platform produces useful insights that help governmental agencies, business enterprises, and environmental researchers manage and conserve the ocean sustainably. This novel approach is economical and scalable for a range of applications involving undersea research.

Our Technology

Bio Mimicry :

Devices resembling fish equipped with cameras and multispectral sensors can survey coastal areas, mapping habitats and detecting changes in vegetation, and pollution levels.

AI Algorithm :

Machine learning algorithms are applied to remote sensing data can automate the analysis of vast datasets. They can identify coral bleaching hotspots, predict algal blooms, and assess the health of seagrass beds based on spectral signatures.

Satellite Imagery :

High-resolution satellite sensors can detect changes in sea surface temperature, water clarity etc. Continuous monitoring helps identify coral bleaching events and track changes in habitat distribution over time.
